A Fly on the Wall

Casual musings of a gypsy soul

Smoke

Neverending, seeping, creeping from some unseen place. A shade over the idyllic Seattle sun that seems to last forever. I'm trapped--trapped in a valley of gray. Stifling, choking darkness. Impotence, apathy, questioning. Desire for escape, the passionate hotness of the sun. Longing to reach out through the mist,to touch something, someone. To communicate, to find my voice, to take action. If I scream, will anyone hear me?
The music starts.
Fingers drumming the keyboard absentmindedly, habitually.
Slowly calling me back to life.
Back to the human race.
The choking ceases.
Breathing is easier.
Life renewed through
Music.
